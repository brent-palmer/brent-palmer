Hello, I’m Brent

Currently at Mixpanel, I manage the Product Design organization where we’re enabling teams of all sizes to understand customer data without requiring an analytics background. How might we give brands instant access to customer insights that can improve their business—no statistics degree required?

As Director of Product Design at Fast, our remote product design team built tools to help merchants sell goods online. We sought to make 1-click checkout with Fast easier, and safer than traditional credit card payments.

Before Fast, I was Head of Design at the defunct startup Proof (reconstituted as Jarvis Jasper). Before that, I ran the design practice at TrendKite, helping brands monitor their news coverage. I’ve also lived and managed teams overseas at Carwow in London, making buying (and selling) vehicles in the UK effortless. And at Zendesk in Montpellier, France, my team led the successful UI/UX integration efforts of the locally acquired startup, BIME.

If you’re keen to hear how I approach product strategy and leading teams, I’ve been honored to speak at UX London, WebExpo, and Design Matters conferences. You can also listen to me wax poetic on UI Breakfast, Ran Talks Design, and Inside Marketing Design podcasts.

That’s enough about me. Fancy a resume or view some work samples? Connect with me on LinkedIn or send me an email.
